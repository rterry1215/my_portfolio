the Challenge

Northand Films is a video production company with a flair for adventure and the outdoors. They appreciate the grit and tenacity of individuals (who doesn’t?) and also the unique tales that comes with each project. Our plan for this particular website was simple: How can visiting this site feel like going on a 2 hour hike by yourself, during a storm, wearing blindfolds and no shoes? Easy peasy.
